expr : KEYWORD: VAR IDENTIFIER EQ expr
     | term PLUS term*
     | term MINUS term*


term : factor MUL factor*
     | factor DIV factor*

factor :
       | PLUS factor
       | MINUS factor
       |LPAREN expr RPAREN

power : number (POW factor)*

number : INT
       | FLOAT
       | IDENTIFIER
       | LPAREN expr RPAREN
